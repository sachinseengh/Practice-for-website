Bholaa


It is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language action thriller film directed by and starring Ajay Devgn. Co-starring Tabu and produced by Ajay Devgn FFilms, Reliance Entertainment, T-Series Films and Dream Warrior Pictures, it is a remake of the Tamil film Kaithi (2019).It also features Deepak Dobriyal, Gajraj Rao, Sanjay Mishra, Vineet Kumar and Makarand Deshpande in supporting roles. Principal photography took place from January 2022 to January 2023 in Mumbai, Hyderabad and Varanasi. The film score and soundtrack album are composed by Ravi Basrur. It is scheduled to be released theatrically on 30 March 2023.
