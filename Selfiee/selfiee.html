Bhediya


It is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language comedy-drama film directed by Raj Mehta, which serves as a remake of the 2019 Malayalam film Driving Licence. Produced by Dharma Productions, Prithviraj Productions, Magic Frames and Cape of Good Films, the film stars Akshay Kumar and Emraan Hashmi alongside Diana Penty and Nushrratt Bharuccha . Bollywood superstar Vijay Kumar needs to obtain a new driving license from RTO officer, Om Prakash Agarwal, a diehard fan of Vijay. A misunderstanding escalates into a feud which is played out in front of the entire country It is scheduled to be theatrically released on 24 February 2023.
